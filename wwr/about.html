History

Established in 1985 by John and Sarah Davidson, Adventure Rafting Co. emerged as a leading name in white-water rafting. Driven by a passion for nature and adventure, the Davidsons set out to share the thrill of navigating rapids with the world. Starting with just a few rafts, Adventure Rafting Co. quickly gained recognition for its exhilarating excursions and commitment to safety. Over the years, the company expanded its offerings while maintaining a strong focus on environmental conservation. Today, Adventure Rafting Co. remains a beacon of adventure, offering unforgettable experiences for adventurers of all ages. With its legacy firmly rooted in exploration and excitement, the company continues to inspire future generations to embrace the thrill of the great outdoors.
