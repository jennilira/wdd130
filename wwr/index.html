Established in 1985 by John and Sarah Davidson, Adventure Rafting Co. emerged as a leading name in white-water rafting. Driven by a passion for nature and adventure, the Davidsons set out to share the thrill of navigating rapids with the world .

About Our White Water Rafting Site

Experience the thrill of white water rafting with our expert guides. Our site offers some of the most exciting rapids in the region, perfect for both beginners and seasoned rafters.

Our purpose is to provide an unforgettable adventure while ensuring the safety and satisfaction of our guests. We prioritize safety, quality, and value for money.

We are prepared for your family!

We offer a range of trips to cater to all experience levels, from tranquil family floats to adrenaline-pumping grade 5 rapids. Join us for an all-inclusive experience of the Nile from its source.

Our experienced guides are certified and trained to ensure your safety throughout the adventure. You are in safe hands as you navigate the exhilarating rapids.

Enjoy breathtaking views and serene surroundings as you raft down the Nile. Our trips are designed to provide both excitement and relaxation.
